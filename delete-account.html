Delete Your Account

You can delete your Happy Wick account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below:

  1. 1 Open the Happy Wick app on your device.
  2. 2 Tap the Settings tab (bottom right).
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. 4 Confirm the deletion. Your account and all data will be permanently removed.
This action is permanent. Deleting your account will immediately and permanently remove all your personal data, including your profile, wallet addresses, feedback submissions, and notification settings. This cannot be undone.
